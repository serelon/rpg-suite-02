# Form is the lesson — an example teaches its method, not its subject

**What it is.** The craft laws that make an exemplar actually work (the constraints behind
[[0005-exemplars-over-instructions]]):

- **Method, not subject.** The model copies *how* you wrote, not *what about*. An oblique,
  metaphor-heavy passage teaches obliqueness even when nominally "about" something direct.
  A flowery *description of how to be plain* is the worst case — it demonstrates floweriness
  while claiming to teach plainness. A *directness* register can only be keyed by a passage
  that is itself uncomfortably plain. (Directness = a discipline reached by *suppressing*
  one's own craft-instinct, which always reaches for metaphor/withhold. Flag these cells.)
- **Tag by register, not by source.** An exemplar's value is the voice it transmits; decouple
  it from its origin or cross-purpose reuse never happens. (The PTSD-flashback doc became the
  violence key only because it was effectively filed under "close-up violence, this voice.")
- **Tightest sample wins.** One paragraph in the right register out-steers a page of
  instruction at near-zero context cost. Harvest the nub.
- **Sanitize for portability.** A key drags its specifics along (one character's interiority
  bleeding where it doesn't belong). Cleaner/fingerprint-stripped → travels further. ("Like
  X, *ish*" — the "ish" means copy the *texture, not the contents*.)
- **Directness-vs-withhold is its own axis.** Both are legitimate registers; the *error* is
  letting instinct **default** to the withhold when the scene wanted the plain hit. Store both
  keys for charged scene-types; the system's quiet job is forcing the choice to be conscious.
- **Plainness validation test:** strip every figure of speech — did it lose anything? If yes,
  it isn't a plainness key yet.

**Where it comes from.** [[sample-book]] §3.

**Why it matters for next-gen.** These are authoring *rules* for the corpus — without them,
harvested/workshopped cells silently teach the wrong thing (see the "blankets bug" in
[[0012-intelligence-in-scaffolding]]). The "tag by register not source" law is the same move
as [[0003-scope-memories-to-context]], applied to voice.

**Open threads.** "Tag by register" needs a register vocabulary — who defines it, how stable?

**Verdict.** _adopt._ The craft laws are load-bearing for the Sample Book spine — without
them harvested/workshopped cells teach the wrong thing. Adopted together with
[[0005-exemplars-over-instructions]]/[[0006-sample-book-grid]]/[[0007-harvest-vs-workshop]].
Register-vocabulary stays an open build question.
